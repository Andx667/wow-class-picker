Quick Notes

Does this replace logs/sims?

No. This helps narrow class choice based on your goals. Use logs/sims after you pick a direction.

Why do some recommendations include tradeoffs?

Because TBC class design is role-dependent. Better decisions come from seeing both strengths and pain points.

Can off-meta choices still work?

Yes. In competent groups, player skill and consistency often matter more than raw tier-list position.
